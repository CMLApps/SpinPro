Privacy Policy

Cloud Motion Lab · Last updated April 29, 2026

Spin Pro is built to spin a wheel and keep your business private. This page explains exactly what the app does and does not collect.

What we collect

Nothing personal. The app does not require an account. It does not collect your name, email, location, contacts, photos, or device identifiers for advertising purposes.

What you type into the wheel

The names, choices, raffle entries, dice rolls, and other lists you enter into Spin Pro are stored locally on your device. They are not transmitted, stored on a server, or shared with any third party. If you delete the app, your saved wheels are deleted with it.

Anonymous analytics

The app uses TelemetryDeck for anonymous, privacy-preserving usage analytics. TelemetryDeck collects coarse, non-identifying signals (such as which screen was viewed, whether a spin was started, app version, device type, country) hashed with a daily rotating salt. No persistent device identifier is stored. You can read TelemetryDeck's privacy approach at telemetrydeck.com/privacy.

In-app purchases

Spin Pro offers an optional Pro upgrade as either a weekly auto-renewing subscription with a free introductory trial or a one-time lifetime purchase. Purchases are processed by Apple's App Store. Spin Pro receives only an Apple-issued receipt confirming entitlement. We do not see your Apple ID, your payment details, or your billing address.

Rewarded ads

Spin Pro may show optional rewarded video ads when you choose to unlock a premium feature without paying. These ads are served by Unity Ads. Ad-network SDKs may collect coarse device signals (device model, OS version, IP address truncated to country level, anonymized advertising identifier where permitted) to deliver relevant ads and prevent fraud. Spin Pro does not pass any personal information to ad networks. You can opt out by skipping the ad-gated feature and either using the free option, paying for Pro, or simply declining the ad. We do not request App Tracking Transparency permission and do not track you across other apps or websites.

What we do not do

  • No tracking across apps or websites
  • No selling, renting, or sharing of any personal data
  • No background location, no contacts, no microphone, no camera

Children

Spin Pro is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content. It is suitable for general use, including by children, and complies with COPPA by collecting no personal information from any user.

Changes to this policy

If we change anything material, the "Last updated" date above will move and we will note the change in the app's What's New on its next release.
